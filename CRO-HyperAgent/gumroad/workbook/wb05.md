## Chapter 5 Workbook — You Are the Business

---

### The Takeaway

The sale begins the moment someone picks up the phone — not when the patient sits in the chair. The front desk is not a support role; it is the first act of building trust. Every nervous caller is deciding, in the first thirty seconds, whether this is a place they can trust with something they are embarrassed about. Your voice, your energy, and your genuine warmth are doing work that no device demonstration can undo or redo.

### The Drill — The Mirror Practice

This is not metaphorical. It is a concrete, daily habit.

**Step 1 — The physical mirror check.**

Before your next ten incoming calls, place a mirror (or use your phone camera) where you can see your face before you answer. For each call, glance at your face. Are you smiling? If not, smile first, then answer.

After ten calls, record what you noticed:
