## Chapter 4 Workbook — You Hear With Your Brain, Not Your Ears

---

### The Takeaway

Hearing aids restore sound, but the brain needs roughly a month to relearn how to interpret that sound. Patients who give up in week two are not failing — they were never prepared for the rewiring process. Your job is to hold the line with them: to say "I've got you" and mean it, week after week, until the breakthrough arrives.
