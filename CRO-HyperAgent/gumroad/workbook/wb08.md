## Chapter 8 Workbook — Thinkers, Knowers, Feelers

---

### 1. The Takeaway

People do not all receive information the same way. Roughly 15% of the people in your chair are Thinkers (they need data), 30% are Knowers (they need highlights), and 55% are Feelers (they need a story). A master communicator does not have one great pitch — they read the person across from them and adapt in the moment. The key is developing your own awareness first: knowing your dominant modality, so you can see when it is pulling you toward the wrong approach.
