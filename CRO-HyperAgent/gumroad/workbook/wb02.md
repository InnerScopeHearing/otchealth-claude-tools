## Chapter 2 Workbook — Folding Mailers & Building Hearing Aids

---

### 1. The Takeaway

Doing more things does not produce more results. Mark discovered this the hard way: the more hearing aids he built himself, the less time he had to sell, and the funnel ran dry. In any practice where the relationship is the product, the most valuable resource is undivided attention — and every task that competes for that attention costs more than it appears to save. The principle applies to every role: map where your time actually goes, and protect the hours that only *you* can fill.
