### 1. The Takeaway

The hearing aid is a tool. What changes a person's life is the relationship — the patience, the follow-through, the honest conversation when they wanted to quit, the gold sticker when they made it one more week. Mark stayed for twenty-three years not because of the business he built, but because of the gifts he was able to give: one person, one moment, one set of returned sounds at a time. Kim heard a grandmother say *"I could hear my granddaughter and her wedding"* and had to take a breath. That is the whole thing. That is why this work is worth doing — and why, when you do it with your whole heart, it is one of the most meaningful things one human being can do for another.
