## 1. What it is
"From the Chair" — a book + fillable Workbook built from the Moore family's real hearing-care career.
Mark Moore = the sales/ownership spine; Kim Moore = the care/front-desk spine. Both are Licensed
Hearing Aid Dispensers (NEVER "audiologist"). One body of content, an AI-Layer bridge in every chapter.

## 2. The TWO EDITIONS (corrected by Matt 2026-06-29)
BOTH editions are for INDUSTRY/PRACTICE PROFESSIONALS (NOT patients/families — that earlier framing was wrong).
Same core content, repackaged/reframed for two mindsets:
- EDITION A — The Closer (Sales/Owner): business owners, salespeople, commission-based dispensers; sales-oriented. Mark's energy. Language: close, win the room, conversion, commission, growth.
- EDITION B — The Professional (Care/Patient-Experience): front desk, call center, support staff, dispensers/audiologists who do NOT want to "sell," who lead with professionalism/care. Kim's energy. Language: patient care, trust, outcomes, professional excellence.
KEY INSIGHT: the techniques are IDENTICAL; only the vocabulary changes. Edition B lets the anti-sales
professional adopt the same practice-growing mechanics without ever using the word "sell."
SELL each edition separately + offer a BUNDLE price.

## 3. Pricing (recommended; Matt-gated, not final)
$79 single edition · $129 both-editions bundle (hero SKU for a whole practice) · $49 launch/seed price.
Later upsell: multi-seat "Practice License" $299-499. Priced as professional development, justified by a substantial workbook.

## 4. The Workbook = the value AND the anti-piracy moat
At a real price, the book is the story and the WORKBOOK is the value. ~70-110 pp of fillable work.
Anti-piracy: a blank PDF is near-worthless; value only exists once filled with the reader's own
practice/patients/numbers -> personal, effortful, pointless to copy. Reinforce with per-buyer
personalization (name + license # on cover, Gumroad license key, light watermark).
Per-chapter template (6 parts): 1) The Principle (+ a Mark/Kim quote) 2) Where I Am Now (self-score)
3) Make It Mine (script/decision tool) 4) Try It This Week (drill + real-patient log) 5) Measure It
(metric tracker) 6) Reflect & Commit. Exercise toolkit: self-score, script builder, real-patient log,
this-week drill, decision tool, metric tracker, role-play card, reflect & commit. Same template both
editions; prompts get the vocabulary swap.
