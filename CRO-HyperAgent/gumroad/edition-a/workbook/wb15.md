# Chapter 15 Workbook — Why They Came Back

---

## The Takeaway

Patients return year after year — and send their neighbors, siblings, and friends — not because of the technology but because someone in your office treated their story as worth knowing. You might be the only person that patient talks to all week. When you listen with genuine curiosity, remember what they told you, and greet them six months later exactly where they left off, you stop being a provider and become part of their life. That is the most durable referral engine there is.
