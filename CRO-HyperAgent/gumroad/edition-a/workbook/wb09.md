## Chapter 9 Workbook — The Testing Chair

---

### 1. The Takeaway

The testing appointment is not a prelude to the real work — it is where the real relationship begins. The patient sitting across from you has often been putting this moment off for years. How you welcome them into that chair, how you frame what is about to happen, and how you deliver the results afterward either builds trust or breaks it. A small, genuine gesture — Kim's Hershey bar, a quiet "don't shoot the messenger" — can crack open a conversation that no clinical protocol could. The test is where caring happens at the most fundamental level.
