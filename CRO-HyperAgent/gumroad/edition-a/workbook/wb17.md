## Chapter 17 Workbook — Winning Without Selling

---

### 1. The Takeaway

Pushy comes from pressure — wanting the outcome for yourself more than you want it for the patient. When your focus is entirely on the patient's well-being, there is no pressure; there is only care. Kim's one-sentence method: treat each patient the way you would treat your parents or grandparents, and you will never come across as pushy. The technique is not a script — it is a posture. And patients who have lived seven or eight decades can feel the difference between someone who genuinely cares and someone who is performing interest in order to get somewhere.
