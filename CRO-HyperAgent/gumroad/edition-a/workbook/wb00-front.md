## How to Use This Workbook

This is not a reading companion. It is a 90-day operating system for your practice.

Here is how the system works. On Day 1, complete the Practice Self-Assessment Scorecard on the next pages — every row, honestly. That baseline score is your starting line, not a judgment. Then work through each of the 18 chapter modules in order. Each module takes roughly 20–30 minutes: you read the takeaway, score yourself on that skill today, reflect with real prompts, build or refine a script, and log what happened with actual patients this week. Do not skip the patient log. That is where this workbook earns its value — a filled-in log of your real patients, your real words, your real outcomes cannot be copied from anyone else. It is yours.

At Day 30, return to the back matter and re-score the full scorecard. Do it again at Day 60 and Day 90. The re-score is not a test — it is a mirror. It shows you what has moved and where to keep your attention.

**Three rules.**

One: fill this in for your practice. The patients, scripts, and numbers that belong in these blanks are yours — not a colleague's, not a hypothetical. Specificity is what makes it work.

Two: keep it private. A workbook you have filled in with your own patients, your own scripts, and your own honest self-scores is your personal playbook. It represents months of deliberate work. It cannot be replicated by anyone who did not do the work.

Three: finish what you start. A workbook abandoned at Chapter 6 is a self-assessment, not an operating system. The 90-day arc is designed so that each part builds on the last — the close depends on the contact, the contact depends on understanding the person, and all of it depends on knowing your why.

Your why is on the next page. Start there.
