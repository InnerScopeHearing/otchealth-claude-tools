## Chapter 11 Workbook ‚Äî You're Not Selling a Hearing Aid, You're Selling Yourself

**The takeaway:** The device is a tool. You are the service. When a patient truly understands that what they are purchasing is your expertise, your continuity, and your commitment to stay with them through every hard stretch of the adaptation process, the price conversation shifts ‚Äî because they are no longer comparing products on a shelf. They are deciding whether to trust a person. Sell the only thing that cannot be replicated at another office: yourself.

### 3 ‚Äî The Drill: Write Your One-Price-Includes-Me Line

This is the single most important sentence you will build in this module. It is the line you say ‚Äî genuinely, in your own voice ‚Äî when a patient has heard the price and is weighing whether it is worth it. It must be true, personal, and specific to you. It cannot be generic. It will not work if it sounds like a script someone handed you.

Use the blanks to build your version.
