## Chapter 16 Workbook — Hiring for Heart

---

### 1. The Takeaway

The most important hire you will ever make is not the most credentialed candidate — it is the one who already carries genuine warmth toward older people in their bones. Systems, scheduling, and procedures can be taught in a week. The kind of patience that lets a 78-year-old take his time telling his story before he sits in the testing booth cannot be taught at all. One honest question — and the willingness to listen carefully to the answer — tells you more than any resume.
