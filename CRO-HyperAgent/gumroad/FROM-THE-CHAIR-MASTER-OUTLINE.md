# From the Chair — Master Outline (Book Bible)

One book · TWO EDITIONS (Patients & Families · Hearing Professionals) · eBook + fillable Workbook · an AI-Layer bridge in every chapter.
Built from Mark Moore's 3-part interview (sales/ownership) + Kim Moore's interview (care/front desk). Both Licensed Hearing Aid Dispensers (NOT audiologists).

## Concept
Every patient sat in two chairs: Kim's front-desk/testing chair (made them safe, learned who they were) and Mark's sales chair (earned trust, helped them decide). Thesis: you don't sell a hearing aid, you help a person; people don't want to be sold, they want to be cared for. Write the chapters once; frame twice.

## Two editions
- A. Patients & Families — "A Family's Guide to Hearing Again." Reassurance + roadmap + how to support a loved one. Closes each chapter "If this is you / If this is someone you love."
- B. Hearing Professionals — "The Moore Method for Caring Practices." Teachable system + front-desk/culture playbook. Carries the fillable Workbook. Closes each chapter "Put it to work Monday."

## The Moore Method (combined)
MARK (Selling Is Serving): sell yourself not the device; people want to buy not be sold; four objections inverted (denial>stigma>vanity>cost); Thinkers/Knowers/Feelers modalities; "what brings you in today?" + repeat-back; price = device + me, cost of inaction, today is best day; a return = the patient returning YOU; duplicatable patient journey process + modified COSI (3 listening goals).
KIM (Care Is the System): caring starts on the first phone call; you are the business (smile before you answer); brain not ears + the month + "I've got you"; rituals (Hershey bar, gold stickers); the loved-one demo; save a return by finding the one issue; hire for heart ("relationship with your parents/grandparents?"); treat every patient like your own family.
