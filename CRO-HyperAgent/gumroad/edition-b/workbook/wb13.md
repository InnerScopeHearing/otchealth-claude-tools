# Chapter 13 Workbook — The Patient Journey

---

## The Takeaway

A fitting is not finished at delivery — it has barely begun. When you and your patient write down exactly three specific listening goals, sign them, and grade every follow-up against those goals, you transform a vague hope into a shared contract. The patient who owns their goals stays in the journey; the patient whose goals were never named is the one who shows up weeks later with a white envelope.
