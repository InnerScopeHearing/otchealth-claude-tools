## Chapter 7 Workbook — Denial, Stigma, Vanity, Cost

---

### 1. The Takeaway

When a patient says "it's too expensive," they are almost never talking about money. The four objections patients give you — cost, vanity, stigma, denial — are stated in reverse order of what is actually blocking them. Denial is the real root; cost is the polite exit. Once you learn to read which layer you are actually working with, you stop applying the wrong solution to the wrong barrier and start having real conversations.
