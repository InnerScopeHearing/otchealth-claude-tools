# storefront-cro — make the store convert

OTCHealthMart is live but pre-revenue: catalog mostly draft, Stripe not connected,
inventory not listed. This skill turns it into a selling machine for the owned
$2-3M of inventory.

## When to invoke
Listing/optimizing products, running an offer, fixing the funnel, or launching the
clearance sale.

## Prerequisites it checks first (hard gates)
- **Stripe (or a live payment rail) connected** — no cash collected without it.
- **FDA OTC Establishment Registration** obtained before any hearing aid ships.
- Inventory refurbished/packaged (legacy units need battery refurb).

## What it does
- **Activate + optimize listings:** move draft SKUs live; clear titles, benefit-led
  copy (senior-first, no claims), real photos, trust signals. The clearance line at
  $199-299.
- **Offers + merchandising:** bundles, tiered pricing (Sontro premium under
  Soundwave's brand, legacy value tier), limited-time framing on real scarcity.
- **Upsell/cross-sell:** accessories, supplements, the TReO line, warranties.
- **Recover carts:** abandoned-cart flow (hand to lifecycle-crm), exit offers.
- **Landing pages + funnel:** campaign landing pages tied to email/ads; measure
  add-to-cart -> checkout -> purchase and iterate on real data.

## Hard guardrails
- No medical/device/efficacy claims; never claim OTCHealth holds a 510(k); Sontro
  listed only under Soundwave's brand/label.
- Returns/HSA-FSA handled per policy; no PHI captured.
- Securities firewall: store content only, no stock/raise language.
- No em or en dashes in published copy.

## Output
A converting store: priced, listed, offer-ready inventory; funnel measured; revenue
to Finance-Ops.
