# growth-pr — real exposure for real milestones, compliantly

The apps and products are the legitimate, real-value engine of visibility. This
skill turns real milestones into factual press + media coverage. **Read
`templates/securities-firewall.md` first; it governs everything here.**

## When to invoke
A real, shipped milestone worth announcing: an app launch or major feature, a
product line going live on OTCHealthMart, a signed partnership/distribution deal, a
real usage or outcome metric, a localization, a store feature.

## What it does
1. **Draft a factual press release** from `templates/press-release.md`: headline +
   dateline + the real news + supporting facts + a boilerplate "About OTCHealth."
   Past-tense facts over predictions.
2. **Build a media pitch + target list** (relevant senior-health, hearing, consumer
   tech, local press). Personalized, factual, short.
3. **Run the firewall pre-publish checklist.** If the draft contains ANY gated-lane
   term (INND, the stock, 3(a)(10), Southridge, the raise, reverse split), it
   STOPS and routes to `ir-support` + attorney review. It does not publish public-
   company content.
4. **Prep distribution** (newswire/PR service, owned channels) — a human sends.

## Hard guardrails (from the firewall)
- No share-price references, predictions, or timing to price. Ever.
- Forward-looking statements get the PSLRA safe-harbor disclaimer.
- Section 17(b): disclose any compensated promotion.
- No medical/device claims; never claim a 510(k). No em or en dashes.
- Anything touching the public company -> attorney-gated, never autonomous.

## Output
A ready-to-review release + pitch + target list, with the firewall checklist
attached. Human approves and sends. Log the announcement for the Exposure agent.
