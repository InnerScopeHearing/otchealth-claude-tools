# digital-products — fast, clean cash with no landmines

The one lane that is safe to run nearly autonomously and can put **first dollars in
on launch day**: downloadable info products. No PHI, no device claims, no securities
surface, no inventory.

## When to invoke
A fast, low-risk cash source is wanted, or a subject-matter expert (e.g. Mark's
Cardinal Health / Cencora pharmacy experience) has knowledge worth packaging.

## What it does
1. **Pick a product** with real demand and low competition (the vetted idea:
   pharmacy/OTC **compliance SOP templates** for category management, hearing-aid
   placement compliance, planogram execution, inventory audits).
2. **Write it** from the expert's verbal/outline input: 10-15 polished SOP templates,
   $49-149 each, in clean PDF/DOCX.
3. **List + auto-deliver** on Gumroad (or similar): product pages, pricing, instant
   delivery, license terms. ~12-15 hrs setup, then ~30 min/week.
4. **Promote** via `content-engine` (factual, no claims) and the email list.

## Economics
First dollars on launch day; modeled ~$1K-5K/month passive at maturity. Near-zero
COGS, no inventory, no fulfillment.

## Guardrails
- Sell information/templates, not medical advice. No diagnosis, no device efficacy
  claims, no "FDA-cleared."
- SOPs are generic best-practice templates, not a specific company's confidential
  procedures; include a "adapt to your own legal/regulatory requirements" disclaimer.
- No em or en dashes in published copy. No securities content (open lane only).

## Output
A live storefront with products that sell + auto-deliver, promoted to the list;
revenue tracked by Finance-Ops.
