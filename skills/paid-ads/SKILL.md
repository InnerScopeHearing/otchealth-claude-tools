# paid-ads — buy demand profitably, when there's cash to deploy

Paid is the scalable demand lever once owned channels (email, content, the store)
are converting. Spend only against a known CAC < margin, and start small.

## When to invoke
A campaign with budget to deploy, scaling a winning offer, or building a raise
reservation list.

## What it does
- **Strategy + structure:** channel pick per goal (Meta/Google for hearing-aid
  demand, Amazon for the marketplace, retargeting the warm 85K), campaign/ad-set
  structure, budgets.
- **Creative:** ad variants via the `designer` skill; senior-first hooks; systematic
  creative testing.
- **Tracking:** pixels/conversions API, UTMs, post-purchase attribution into PostHog;
  optimize to ROAS/CAC and contribution margin, not clicks.
- **Reservation funnel:** for a launch/raise, drive cheap reservations (email capture)
  that convert far higher than cold traffic.

## Hard guardrails
- **Ad-platform health policy:** hearing aids/medical are restricted categories;
  no efficacy/diagnosis claims; comply with each platform's medical-device rules;
  never claim a 510(k) OTCHealth doesn't hold.
- **FTC:** truthful, substantiated claims; clear disclosures (#ad on influencer/paid).
- **Securities firewall:** do NOT run paid ads that promote INND or solicit
  investment, securities advertising (Reg D 506(c) general solicitation, Reg CF) has
  strict rules and is **counsel + ir-support territory, never an autonomous ad buy.**
- No PHI in audiences/targeting. No em or en dashes in ad copy.

## Output
Live, tracked campaigns optimized to CAC/ROAS; spend + return on the cash scoreboard;
losers cut fast.
