# content-engine — compounding organic exposure

Steady, factual content compounds the apps' and store's visibility over time, the
legitimate way to build exposure. **Obeys `growth-pr/templates/securities-firewall.md`.**

## When to invoke
Ongoing marketing content: social posts, SEO/blog articles (hearing health, senior
wellness, product how-tos), short-form video scripts, newsletter sections,
community/forum answers.

## What it does
- **Plan + draft** factual, helpful, senior-first content per brand profile.
- **Produce visuals/video** via the `designer` skill (on-brand by construction).
- **Repurpose** one asset across channels (blog -> social -> short video -> email).
- **SEO** for the topics seniors actually search (hearing loss signs, OTC hearing
  aids, tinnitus relief), driving organic discovery of the apps and store.

## Guardrails
- **Educational framing only:** "may help" language, never diagnosis, treatment
  claims, efficacy promises, or "FDA-cleared."
- **Securities firewall:** content is product/brand only. Any drift toward INND, the
  stock, a raise, or share price -> stop, route to ir-support + attorney review.
- No PHI in any content or prompt. No em or en dashes in published copy.
- Disclose any compensated placement (Section 17(b)) if applicable.

## Output
A content calendar + ready-to-publish pieces with assets; human approves brand-voice
sensitive items; performance tracked in PostHog.
