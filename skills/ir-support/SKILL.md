# ir-support — IR done right, gated by counsel and Matt

This is the **gated lane** of the securities firewall. It exists so investor
communication is **factual, compliant, and consistent**, never promotional. It
**drafts only**; a human (Matt) and counsel approve before anything is sent or filed.

## When to invoke
A shareholder update is due, an inbound IR question arrives (Roger line / email), the
IR newsletter needs drafting, or the raise (Reg D / WeFunder) needs factual investor
materials.

## What it does (draft-only)
- **Factual shareholder updates** about real, already-public or properly-disclosable
  business developments.
- **IR FAQ + inbound-response drafts** from the approved IR template library (the
  21 Reg-FD-defensive templates), tone-matched, Tier-3 draft-only.
- **Raise support:** factual data-room and investor-update drafts (Reg D 506(c)
  requires accredited verification; WeFunder requires Form C + audited financials).

## Hard rules (the reason this skill is gated)
- **Reg FD:** no selective disclosure of material non-public information. Material
  news goes out broadly via a filed/issued release, not to a subset.
- **No promotion:** no share-price references, no "undervalued / buy / about to
  move," no language designed to drive liquidity. Factual disclosure only.
- **Forward-looking statements:** PSLRA safe-harbor + cautionary language.
- **Section 17(b):** disclose any compensated IR/promotion.
- **Affiliate awareness:** the Moore family are affiliates; nothing here advises on
  or facilitates trading (Rule 144 is counsel's domain).
- **Never autonomous:** every output is draft-only -> attorney review -> Matt
  approves -> then sent/filed. The tool cannot publish IR content itself.

## Output
A counsel-ready draft + the firewall checklist + a clear "ATTORNEY REVIEW REQUIRED"
flag. Logged for the IR/Capital agent. Nothing leaves without human + legal sign-off.
