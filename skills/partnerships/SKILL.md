# partnerships — scale demand through real channels

Distribution and referral partnerships turn the products into volume. This skill
runs the outreach and tracks the pipeline.

## When to invoke
Opening or advancing a distribution/demand channel: pharmacy/retail buyers, senior-
living operators, Amazon onboarding, audiologist/clinic referrals, ambassador or
affiliate/referral programs.

## What it does
- **Target + research** the right partners and buyers per channel.
- **Draft factual, value-based outreach** (what the product is, the margin/terms, why
  it fits their shelves/residents/patients). No medical efficacy claims.
- **Manage the pipeline** (stages, follow-ups via Customer.io/n8n) and prep the
  materials (line sheets, terms, compliance docs the buyer needs: 510(k) numbers held
  by partners, FCC/UL, barcodes for Amazon).
- **Referral/ambassador programs:** structure and instrument a referral loop to the
  existing customer base (a near-term, low-cost demand lever).

## Guardrails
- **Channel compliance:** Amazon medical-device gate (list as OTC hearing aids, not
  amplifiers; PHC gate; UDI/GS1 barcodes). Sontro sold only under Soundwave's brand;
  never claim OTCHealth holds a 510(k).
- No medical claims; no PHI in outreach. No em or en dashes in published copy.
- Product/commercial lane only. Any investor/stock angle -> ir-support + attorney.

## Output
A live partner pipeline with drafted outreach + materials; deals handed to Commerce
to fulfill; referral program instrumented and tracked.
