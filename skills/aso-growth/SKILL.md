# aso-growth — organic app visibility, the real funnel

Apps are the slow-but-real exposure engine. This skill maximizes their organic
reach so the visibility is earned, not bought.

## When to invoke
A store listing needs creating/optimizing, a launch needs ASO, or ratings/installs
need growing for iHEARtest, AWARE, Companion, InnerEase, MedReview.

## What it does
- **Store listings:** title, subtitle, keyword field, description tuned per app and
  audience. Senior-first language; no medical/efficacy claims; no em or en dashes.
- **Visual assets:** App Store screenshots + preview video via the `designer` skill,
  with headline overlays and device frames.
- **Ratings + reviews:** in-app review prompts at the right moment (post-success),
  respond to reviews, surface fixes to the product team.
- **Localization:** reuse i18n (iHEARtest has Arabic) to widen reach.
- **Measurement:** track impressions -> product-page-views -> installs -> activation
  (via PostHog), and iterate the listing on real data.

## Guardrails
- No medical claims, no diagnosis language, no implied clinical credentials.
- Senior accessibility in the copy (plain language, large value props).
- No em or en dashes in any published store copy.
- ASO is product marketing (open lane). It never references the public company,
  the stock, or any securities matter (see growth-pr/templates/securities-firewall.md).

## Output
Optimized listings + assets live in the store; performance tracked; iteration queued.
