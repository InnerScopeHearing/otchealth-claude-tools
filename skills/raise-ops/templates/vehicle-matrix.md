# Raise-vehicle decision matrix (orient with counsel; this is not legal advice)

| Vehicle | Who can invest | Cap | Solicitation | Financials needed | Time-to-cash | Best for |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Reg D 506(c)** | Accredited only (issuer must VERIFY) | none | General solicitation allowed | Data room / DD | **days-weeks** | Fast capital from accredited/institutional. OTCHealth's live Series C. |
| **Reg D 506(b)** | Accredited + ≤35 sophisticated, pre-existing relationship | none | No general solicitation | Data room | weeks | Quiet rounds with known investors |
| **Reg CF (WeFunder)** | Anyone (limits by income) | ~$5M/yr | Allowed (+ TTW) | Form C + reviewed/audited | **~8-12 wk prep** | Broad retail crowd, community |
| **Reg A+ (Tier 2)** | Anyone | ~$75M/yr | Allowed | SEC-qualified offering circular, audited | **months** | Larger later-stage "mini-IPO" |

## Decision rules
1. **Need cash fastest, have accredited contacts?** -> Reg D 506(c) (the live lever).
2. **Want the crowd + a marketing flywheel, can wait 8-12 weeks?** -> Reg CF / WeFunder.
3. **Raising big and later-stage?** -> Reg A+.
4. You can run **506(c) now + WeFunder in parallel** (different investor pools); the
   OTCHealth plan pairs a $1M Reg D with a $500K CF track.

## Pre-launch checklist (every vehicle)
- [ ] Counsel engaged; Bad Actor (Rule 503) checks done
- [ ] Financials at the required level (reviewed/audited as the vehicle demands)
- [ ] Data room ready (NDA-gated for institutional DD)
- [ ] Reservation list built (target 1,500-3,000 before launch)
- [ ] Every investor-facing asset attorney + Matt approved
- [ ] Accredited-verification process in place (506(c))
- [ ] PSLRA safe-harbor language on any forward-looking statements
- [ ] No share-price promotion anywhere; 17(b) disclosure if compensated promotion
- [ ] No 510(k) misstatement; no medical claims; no em or en dashes
