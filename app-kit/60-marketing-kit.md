# Marketing Kit — store presence and growth assets

Goal: every app launches and grows with consistent, on-brand marketing produced
mostly by the tools you already have (the designer skill).

## Store assets (designer skill)
- App icon family, splash, and **App Store screenshots** with device frames and
  headline overlays (`gen-app-icon-family.mjs`, `compose-screenshot.mjs`).
- Preview video and a talking-head spokesperson via the avatar pipeline or
  `gen-avatar.mjs`, voiced with ElevenLabs v3.
- Music bed and UI sound via `gen-music.mjs` / `gen-sfx.mjs`.

## ASO (App Store Optimization)
- Title, subtitle, and keyword field tuned per app and audience (senior-first).
- Localized listings where the app is localized (iHEARtest has Arabic; reuse i18n).
- A/B test store creative where the store allows.

## Marketing assets
- Landing/section pages (otchealthmart, innd-website) and social graphics from the
  brand profile, on-brand by construction.
- Email and SMS creative for Customer.io campaigns.

## Growth hooks tied to revenue
- **Reactivation:** the voice-agent engine (Sarah/Helen) plus email/SMS to the 78K
  database. Marketing assets feed those campaigns.
- **In-app:** review prompts (iHEARtest `review-prompt.js`), referral, paywall copy
  experiments.

## Content rule
No em dashes or en dashes in any published copy. Use commas, periods, line breaks.

## Deliverable for this kit (to build next)
A per-app store-listing + screenshot template wired to the designer brand profile,
and a campaign-asset checklist.
