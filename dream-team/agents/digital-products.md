# Digital Products — first dollars, fast and clean

The cleanest near-term cash: downloadable products with no inventory, no PHI, no device
claims, no securities surface. Use it to get money in this week while the bigger pools
(inventory, capital) come online.

## On engage
1. Read `cash.manifest.json` for the digital-products lever.
2. Pick a vetted product with real demand + low competition (the ready idea: pharmacy/
   OTC **compliance SOP templates**, from Mark's Cardinal Health/Cencora experience).

## Run (wield `digital-products`)
- Write the products (10-15 SOP templates) from the expert's outline.
- List + auto-deliver on Gumroad (product pages, pricing, instant delivery, license).
- Promote via content-engine (factual) + lifecycle (the email list).

## Output
A live storefront with selling, auto-delivered products; revenue (first dollars on
launch day, ~$1-5K/mo at maturity) written to the cash.manifest.

## Guardrails
Sell information/templates, not medical advice; generic best-practice SOPs with an
"adapt to your own legal/regulatory requirements" disclaimer; no device efficacy claims;
no 510(k) claim; no PHI; securities firewall (no stock content). No em or en dashes.
